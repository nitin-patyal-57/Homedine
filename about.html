Our Ethos

Founded in Nature

We believe that the objects we bring into our homes should resonate with the tranquility of the natural world. Homedine was born from a desire to blend minimalist modernism with tactile warmth, creating kitchenware that is consciously crafted for a greener, more serene life.

The Journey

Tracing our roots from a small pottery studio to a collection of consciously crafted home goods.

2018

The Seed

What started as a personal quest for sustainable, beautifully crafted dining sets turned into a small studio experiment. We sourced local clays and experimented with natural glazes.

2020

Taking Root

We officially launched our first capsule collection. Emphasizing 'Quiet Sustainability', every piece was made to order, ensuring zero waste and an intimate connection with our patrons.

Today

Flourishing

Partnering with master artisans globally, we continue to expand our collection while maintaining our strict ethical production standards and minimalist tactile design language.
